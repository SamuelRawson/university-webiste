UNIVERSITY OF DELTA, P.M.B 2090, AGBOR, DELTA STATE.

SCREENING OF UTME CANDIDATES FOR 2022/2023 ADMISSIONS EXERCISE.
The University of Delta,Agbor, hereby announces the screening of UTME candidates for admission into the Institution for the 2022/2023 Academic Session. Applications are only open to candidates that scored 140 and above at the 2022 UTME examinations and have completed the procedures for change of their Universities of First Choice to the University of Delta, Agbor.

FACULTIES ARE :

(A) FACULTY OF ARTS

(B) FACULTY OF EDUCATION

(C) FACULTY OF ENGINEERING

(D) FACULTY OF ENVIRONMENTAL SCIENCES

(E) FACULTY OF COMPUTING

(F) FACULTY OF LAW

(G) FACULTY OF MANAGEMENT AND SOCIAL SCIENCES

(H) FACULTY OF SCIENCES

GENERAL ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS:

Candidates must possess five(5) credit passes in the relevant subjects in the SSCE, NECO or GCE O/L Examinations at not more than two sittings as well as other matriculation requirements stipulated by JAMB.

SCHOOL FEE PAYMENT PROCEDURE:

1. Login with your User Name or id and Password
2. Once you login, click on "My Data" a drop down opens
3. Click on "Payments" 4. Click on "Add current session payment ticket"
5. On the "Add online Payment Category"select "School Fee" on the drop down
6. Then Click on "Create ticket"
7. Click on "Pay via Interswitch CollegePAY"
8. On the "Submit data to CollegePAY" page click on "Submit"
9. Enter your card details on the Interswitch webpay page and click on "Pay"
10.Download the payment receipt after a successful payment has been made

Note: Your CVV2 is the 3- digit number printed at the back of your card.
